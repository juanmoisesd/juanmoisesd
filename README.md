## 🔬 About Me

Researcher and data scientist focused on **Latin American public health data**, open science, and reproducible research. I build and maintain open datasets covering neuroscience, mental health, epidemiology, and social determinants of health across Latin America and Ibero-America.

- 🧠 **Research areas**: Neuroscience · Mental Health · Epidemiology · AI in Research · Digital Health
- 📊 **92+ datasets** published across Harvard Dataverse, Zenodo, Mendeley Data, and Figshare
- 🌎 **Coverage**: 22+ Latin American and Ibero-American countries
- 🐍 **Open tools**: Python packages for programmatic data access
- 📖 **Open science**: All datasets under CC-BY-4.0 or CC0
